TYPICAL MAIN ROOF STRUCTURE                      The main roof structure is about 175m wide by
                                                 396m long on plan. It is 27m high at the eaves, rising
                                                 to 39m at centre span. It has a clear span of 156m
                                                 across the width of the building.

                                                 The main roof structure is independent of the
                                                 terminal's superstructure floors.

                                                 Main roof rafters and associated tie-rods are at 18m
                                                 centres, supported by 'tree' assemblies at 36m
                                                 centres, which in turn are supported at ground floor
                                                 level at 18m centres.
